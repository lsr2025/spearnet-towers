Company Overview

Building the
Backbone

Spearnet Towers (Pty) Ltd is a pioneering South African telecommunications infrastructure company dedicated to developing, deploying and managing innovative, efficient cellphone tower solutions.

Guided by the President's vision for a digitally empowered South Africa, Spearnet enhances mobile network coverage — particularly across underserved and remote regions. We believe robust digital connectivity is essential infrastructure: the foundation for economic growth, social inclusion and stronger public services.

Our Offering

What We Do

Infrastructure that operators, government and communities can build on — literally.

Primary Revenue Stream

Tower Leasing

Multi-tenant leasing engineered to maximise profitability and efficiency. Pricing is structured around tower specifications, location and tenant volume — so a single structure can serve multiple operators at once.

Built-to-Suit

Custom tower construction plus long-term leasing for operators expanding into underserved markets — reducing capital expenditure and accelerating deployment.

Government & Private Contracts

Public-private partnerships, grants and service-level agreements that deliver connectivity to schools, clinics and government offices.

Infrastructure Sharing & Co-location

Hosting third-party equipment on Spearnet towers — for government bodies, smart-city initiatives and private networks.

Emerging Stream

Data & Analytics Services

Turning network and site infrastructure into insight — coverage analytics, utilisation data and intelligence that helps operators and partners plan smarter rollouts.
